A Minor 'Tail' About My Dogs

Besides the formal introduction, I have no idea what to write about for this blog. So, to make it seem less "empty" I'll just talk about my dogs. When I was about 7 years old, I got my first dog. He was and is a white chihuahua with brown spots all over. At the time, he was only a puppy, but he was very energetic. He would run all over the house and even managed to climb over most chain-link fences, which became an issue. A year passes, and he still is as energetic as he was. The only difference now was that we were getting complaints about his "spiderlike" tendencies. To try and calm his tendencies, we got another puppy. A blonde and fluffy chihuahua, who for some reason could not bark. They got used to each other surprisingly quickly and would play for hours. The complaints stopped, and now they are both two elderly dogs who seem to just nag at each other with their old age. Since then, we have gotten another two dogs who are less than 3 years old. They are definetely much larger, a beagle and husky-mix, but are much more "talkative." They bark more than the last, so thats another set of problems to face.
